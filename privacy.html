Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

At Image Resizer 20kb (hosted on OrbitWebTools), your privacy is our #1 priority. We understand that your personal photos and signatures are sensitive.

1. No Server Uploads (Client-Side Processing)

We use advanced Client-Side JavaScript technology. This means:

  • Your photos NEVER leave your phone or computer.
  • Your images are NOT uploaded to any cloud server.
  • All compression happens directly inside your web browser.

2. Data Collection

Since we do not have a backend server, we do not (and cannot) collect, store, or share your personal images.

3. Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. The tool is designed for instant use without tracking.
